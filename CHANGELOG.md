## 0.5.3

### Changed

- Skins screen redesigned as a cinematic display. The 3D model is centered and front-and-center, with a hexagonal pedestal beneath it like a figurine stand. Controls float over the canvas and fade away after a moment of inactivity, leaving just the model. Saved skins and capes sit in side docks that hug the model. The model strikes a calm idle pose instead of walking, and the whole view scales with the window so it stays large on big displays.
